Capgemini Hiring Process – B.E./B.Tech Freshers (2025)

1. Application
- Apply via Capgemini Careers portal, campus placement drives, or referral programs.
- Resume should highlight programming skills, academic projects, and any relevant certifications.

2. Online Assessment
- Includes sections on Quantitative Aptitude, Logical Reasoning, Verbal Ability, and Essay Writing.
- Coding Test:
  - 2 programming questions (languages allowed: C, C++, Java, Python).
  - Duration: ~60 minutes for technical rounds.
- Some drives include a Game-Based Aptitude Test.

3. Technical Interview
- Covers basic and intermediate programming concepts, data structures, algorithms, DBMS, and OOPs.
- May discuss academic projects, internships, and basic scenario-based problems.

4. HR Interview
- Focuses on communication, teamwork, flexibility, and cultural fit.
- Questions about goals, strengths/weaknesses, work preferences.

5. Offer and Onboarding
- Selected candidates receive offer letters for roles such as Analyst or Software Engineer via email.
- Onboarding includes orientation, systems access, and assignment to training and project groups.

Eligibility Criteria (2025)
- Education: B.E./B.Tech (all branches eligible).
- Minimum 60% marks or 6.0 CGPA in 10th, 12th, and graduation.
- No active backlogs at the time of selection.
- Maximum academic gap allowed: 1 year between any two qualifications.
- Final-year students (2025 batch) are eligible for fresher roles.

Roles Hired For
- Analyst
- Software Engineer
- Graduate Trainee
- IT Support Engineer

Salary Package (2025)
- Analyst/Software Engineer: ₹3.6–₹4.0LPA (Lakhs Per Annum).
- Higher packages (~₹6LPA and above) offered for specialized Digital or advanced technical roles.

Tips for Preparation
- Practice Capgemini-specific aptitude and coding tests.
- Revise programming fundamentals, project explanations, and key CS/IT concepts.
- Prepare for essay writing and game-based aptitude rounds.
- Work on communication and interpersonal skills for interviews.
- Stay updated about Capgemini services, clients, and recent achievements.
